Exploring the Wild: My First Safari Experience

My first safari was an adventure I had eagerly anticipated, thinking I could spot all the animals on my first try. However, the vastness of the jungle soon shattered that expectation. I visited Nagarhole National Park in the Nagarhole Coorg region, and as I entered the jungle, I realized that wildlife was not like a zoological park with animals on display.

The jungle was immense, and spotting animals was far from easy. Despite the challenges, we were rewarded with a spectacular sighting: a playful tusker that entertained us for about 30 minutes. With prayers and a desperate longing to see a big cat, our luck finally changed. We heard alarm calls from a spotted deer, which led us deep into the jungle where we had the chance to see a leopardess and her subadult cub.

This memorable experience was the highlight of my first safari visit and left me with a newfound respect for the wild.
