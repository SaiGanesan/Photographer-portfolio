Monsoon Magic: A Jungle Transformed

My first visit to the jungle was from March to April, a period when the landscape was dry and barren. However, my next trip in June was a complete contrast, as the jungle had transformed into a lush green paradise. The monsoon rains had revived the forest, bringing it to life with herds of elephants, playful langurs, and chital deer joyfully splashing in the rainwater.
